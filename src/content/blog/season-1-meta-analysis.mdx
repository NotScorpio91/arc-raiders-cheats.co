Three weeks into Season 1, patterns are clear. Certain loadouts, routes, and playstyles extract more consistently than others. This is not guesswork — it is based on community data, our own testing, and hundreds of observed raids.

## Dominant Loadout: AR + SMG

The K-47 paired with the Viper-9 SMG is the most common loadout among players who extract 60%+ of their raids. The reason is range coverage — you handle mid-range fights with the AR and close-range emergencies with the SMG without carrying a weapon that is useless in half your engagements.

Heavy armor loadouts are popular on Reddit but underperform in solo statistics. Light armor with high mobility extracts more loot per hour because you spend less time in fights you cannot win and more time moving between loot zones.

## Map Meta: Urban Over Industrial

Urban maps have lower average loot value per crate but higher extraction rates. The compact layout means shorter travel times, more extraction options, and less exposure to long-range snipers.

Industrial maps offer better loot but lower survival rates. The data is clear: new and intermediate players should grind urban maps until their extraction rate hits 50%, then move to industrial for higher profit runs.

## The Early Extract Strategy

The most underrated strategy in Season 1 is extracting early. Players who leave at minute 8–10 with 60% bag capacity earn more per hour than players who push to minute 15 with 100% capacity and die 40% of the time.

Math: 5 extractions at 60% value beats 3 extractions at 100% value when you factor in death losses. Early extract is not cowardly — it is efficient.

## What the Top Players Do Differently

After watching high-extraction-rate players across dozens of raids, three habits stand out:

1. **They check the map every 90 seconds.** Extraction point rotations and player density change constantly. Map awareness prevents panic decisions.

2. **They never fight at full health disadvantage.** If ambushed while looting, they disengage immediately. Ego kills more loot than any weapon.

3. **They have a Plan B extract before they start looting.** When Plan A gets contested, they rotate without hesitation. Indecision at extraction is the number one killer of experienced players.

## Predictions for the Rest of Season 1

Based on current balance and community trends:

- SMG buffs in patch 1.2 will increase close-range rush strategies
- Industrial map loot buffs will shift some players back to high-risk routes
- LMG squad strategies will grow as the movement penalty reduction makes anchors more mobile
